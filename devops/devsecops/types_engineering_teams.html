    • What is software engineering

    • Software engineering is a systematic engineering approach to software development
    • A software engineer is a person who applies the principles of software engineering to design, develop, maintain, test, and evaluate computer software Wikipedia
    • Software engineering also involves considering the non-functional requirements of a software system
      • Performance
      • Security
      • Scalability
    • It also involves working with stakeholders to understand their requirements
    • Includes making design and development decisions that balance trade-offs between cost, time, and quality

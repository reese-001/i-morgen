Benefits of Cloud Computing

  • Benefits of the Cloud
    • Replacing capex with opex
      • Capex requires businesses to invest in and maintain their own infrastructure
      • Opex removes that requirement and allows the infrastructure to be easily scaled to current demand
        • Other benefits of opex model
          • Predictable costs
          • Flexibility
          • Reduce risk
    • Economies of scale
      • Achieve a lower variable cost then you can get on your own
      • Aggregates usage to achieve higher economies of scale
    • Stop guessing capacity
      • Cloud platforms provide elastic and scalable computing resources that can be adjusted based on your needs in near real-time
      • No more over/under provisioning
    • Increase speed and agility
      • Rapid provisioning of new resources
      • Automation
    • Stop spending money running and maintaining data centers
      • Focus on projects that differentiate your business. Focus on customers
    • Go global in minutes
      • Deploy your application in multiple regions and enable customers to access with lower latency

    Types of Cloud Computing

    • Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)
    • Platform as a Service (PaaS)
    • Software as a Service (SaaS)

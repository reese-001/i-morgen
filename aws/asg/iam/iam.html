IAM (Identify Access Management)

What is it?

  • Allows management of access of users and resources
  • Three different IAM Identities
    1. Users: End users who log into the console or interacrt with AWS resources programmatically
    2. Groups: Group up your users so they will all share permission levels of the group (ie Admins, Devs, Auditors)
    3. Roles: Associate permissions to a role and they assn this to users or groups
  • IAM policies: JSON docs which grant permissions for a specific user, group or role to access services. Policies are attached to Identities

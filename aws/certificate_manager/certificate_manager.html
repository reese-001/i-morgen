AWS Certificate Manager - ACM

What is it?

  • Helps provision, manage and deploy public and private SSL TLS certificates
  • Can be used within AWS as well as your internal connected resources
  • With ACM, you can request, renew, and manage SSL/TLS certificates for your websites and applications, and ACM will automatically renew your certificates before they expire
  • You can use ACM to deploy SSL/TLS certificates for a variety of applications, including web servers, load balancers, and email servers
  • To use ACM, you request a certificate, specify the domain or domains that you want to secure, and ACM will handle the rest, including verifying your ownership of the domain and issuing the certificate

  • y = mx + b

    • y = mx + b

    • Can the value of an SRE team be represented by this question?
      • An environment where each new unit of work increases cost by one unit would be represented by y = (1)x + b
        • m = 1
        • b = fixed cost
        • There is nothing to leverage and costs and workload rise in tandom by equal units
      • An environment where each new unit of work increases cost by .5 unit would be represented by y = .5x + b
        • m = .5
        • b = fixed cost
        • There is some automation and costs rise at 1/2 per increase in workload
      • An environment where each new unit of work increases cost by .25 unit would be represented by y = .25x + b
        • m = .25
        • b = fixed cost
        • There is a lot of automation and costs rise at 1/4 per increase in workload
    • The goal becomes two fold
      • Decrease fixed costs b
      • Miminize the increase in cost per increase in work by reducing m
    • The tools to reduce m are the tools of the SRE

    • Overview

    • Technical skills
      • Consider candidates who have strong technical skills in areas such as system administration, network design, cloud and software development
    • Experience with devops methodologies
      • CI/CD, IaC, monitoring, logging
    • Problem solving
      • Look for candidates who have a strong track record of solving challenging technical issues, and who are able to think creatively and outside the box to find solutions
    • Communication skills
      • Look for candidates who are articulate and able to explain technical concepts to non-technical people
    • Availability and on-call responsibilites
      • Consider the candidates' availability and willingness to be on-call when making your hiring decision
    • Cultural fit
      • It's important to ensure that the candidate fits well with the company's culture and values, as well as with the team they will be working with
    • Attitude and approach
      • Look for candidates who have a positive attitude, are open-minded and collaborative, and who are constantly seeking to learn and improve

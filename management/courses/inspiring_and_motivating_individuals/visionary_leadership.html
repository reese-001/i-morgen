Visionary Leadership

The business case for visionary leadership 

  • Why is it so important to have leaders who can create compelling visions that bring people together around a shared direction
  • Leader of a team accounts for 31% of whether that team performs well or not
    • Of that 31%, what are the most critical behaviours that leaders are engaging in that enable teams to perform well
      • Create and communicate the vision
          • Your ability to do this, and create shared vision for the team, is likely the most important behaviour you can engage in
      • Structure of the team (roles, responsibilities, interdependence)
      • Show considersation for individual team members
      • Source: DeRue, et al., Personal Psychology, 2011
  • What is the impact on firm performance in terms of growth/profitability?
    • Your ability to create and commujnicate a vision is most important in times of uncertainty
      • Market is dynamic and changing rapidely
